Most of the photos on this page come from Joe Piggot and Audry Richmond. Joe Piggot is 86 and comes from St Helens. His father was Robert Piggot of Tarbuck Farm and his mother was Salina Atherton of 55 Carr Mill Road, sister of Ellen Atherton who married Robert Dixon. Joe was a cousin of Nelly Littler nee Piggot, who died recently. If anyone can identify the unknown photos please get in touch.
