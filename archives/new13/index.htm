more photos

Most of these photos come from one of the many great grandchildren of Thomas Frodsham and Sarah nee Green, married 7/11/1885 and reputed to have had 20 childen. The sixth child, John, born 11/1/1894, married Mary Rimmer from Church Square, Orell and their son, John, married Francis Cunliffe. The photos marked pic 01-11 are from the Eagle and Child but we don't know wo they are. They are probably related to a former landlord.
