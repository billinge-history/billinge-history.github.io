Billinge History Society

Notice board

History Society meetings are held at the Labor Club on the second Tuesday of the month from September to June. Everyone is more than welcome to attend. Meetings start at 7.30.

Read the full written text at your leisure. It consists of nine parts. Part 7 is about Billinge Chairs and Aidans Church. Part 8 is an important new study relating to the Billinge family and early Billinge history.

You can contact us. Create an issue if you have any inquiries or want anything inserting in this Notice Board.
