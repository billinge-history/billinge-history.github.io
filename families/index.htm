The main body of the written text is not intended to be a final version. We hope you suggest additions and corrections. Better still, we hope you send anything at all concerning Billinge to us to be included in the text. Speak to the old ones and write down their memories before it's too late. The only way to preserve our history is to write it down for future generations. Please let us have your own family history, if possible. We do need your input to add more information, please contribute.

If you require high resolution versions of the photos contained throughout this site just create an issue. We have them saved at 300 ppi resolution. If you have any old photos please create an issue.
