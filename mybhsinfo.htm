Billinge History Society

Our Objectives

  • To preserve the history of Billinge for present and future generations.
  • To promote interest in the history of Billinge.
  • To publish the history of Billinge in printed and electronic formats so that interested parties have easy and unrestricted access to it.

We hold open public meetings on the first Tuesday of the month - all welcome.

If you have any old Billinge photos please let us scan them for posterity. We have saved over 300 historic photos to date - view them in the galleries. We can e-mail you high resolution versions of any of these photos if you want to print them. Perhaps you can come to a meeting sometime.
